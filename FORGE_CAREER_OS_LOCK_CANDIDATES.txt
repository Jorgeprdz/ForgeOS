FORGE CAREER OS LOCK CANDIDATES

Status:
CONSTITUTIONAL LOCK CANDIDATES / NOT APPROVED

Purpose:
List constitutional statements that may become lock candidates after human
review and a separate approval PAQ.

LOCK CANDIDATE 1

Forge is a Career Operating System.

Reason:
The architecture spans Candidate, Precontract, Advisor Development,
Professional Advisor, Manager and Partner stages. CRM is too narrow.

Risk:
Could become marketing language unless tied to lifecycle ownership and decision
outputs.

LOCK CANDIDATE 2

Forge is not a CRM, dashboard collection, database, sales tracker or onboarding
platform.

Reason:
Forge exists to create decisions, actions and development paths, not merely to
store information.

Risk:
Must not deny the existence of CRM-like data needs. Forge can consume records
without being defined by them.

LOCK CANDIDATE 3

Excel stores information. Forge helps build a career.

Reason:
This captures the constitutional distinction between passive storage and
career-oriented intelligence.

Risk:
Needs careful language so it does not become marketing copy.

LOCK CANDIDATE 4

Career stage is a first-class architectural context.

Reason:
Candidate, Precontract, Advisor, Manager and Partner workflows require different
rules, signals and outputs.

Risk:
Requires a canonical Career Lifecycle model and ownership decision.

LOCK CANDIDATE 5

Forge converts career context into decisions and action paths.

Reason:
This preserves Decision Clarity First while expanding from advisor sales into
career progression.

Risk:
Must not imply Forge controls the career or replaces human judgment.

LOCK CANDIDATE 6

Manager Intelligence exists to develop people, not surveil them.

Reason:
Career OS expands manager visibility, so the constitutional boundary must be
explicit.

Risk:
Manager-facing analytics can drift into performance policing if not constrained.

LOCK CANDIDATE 7

Purpose is human context, not a commercial pressure mechanism.

Reason:
Purpose Snapshot and Future You Conversation become more powerful under Career
OS and need protection.

Risk:
Purpose data could be misused for sales pressure, shame or manager coercion.

LOCK CANDIDATE 8

Career OS must preserve evidence, confidence and source ownership.

Reason:
Career recommendations can affect trust, coaching and economic decisions.

Risk:
Without evidence, Career OS becomes narrative guesswork.

LOCK CANDIDATE 9

Forge must never invent career, financial, behavioral or motivational
conclusions.

Reason:
No invented data must extend beyond products and financial values into human and
career interpretation.

Risk:
Generative explanation layers may be tempted to infer too much.

LOCK CANDIDATE 10

Intelligence without career movement is still just information.

Reason:
This adapts the existing Forge principle "Intelligence without action is just
information" to the Career OS constitutional frame.

Risk:
Needs definition of "career movement" so it does not become vague.

LOCK CANDIDATE 11

Business Planning is the operating bridge from ambition to execution.

Reason:
Career OS requires translating purpose, forecast, behavior and production
context into action paths.

Risk:
Could become static planning theater if not tied to live decisions.

LOCK CANDIDATE 12

Alfred is the command surface of Career OS.

Reason:
If Forge is an operating system, Alfred is how the professional moves through
intent, actions and context.

Risk:
Must preserve boundary between Alfred experience and Command OS execution
logic.

LOCK CANDIDATE 13

Benvenù is the first boot of Career OS, not onboarding.

Reason:
This protects Benvenù, Leopard Experience, First Wow Moment, Future You
Conversation and Purpose Snapshot from form-based CRM patterns.

Risk:
Could become theatrical if not connected to first value.

LOCK CANDIDATE 14

Career OS must support multiple professional lifecycle stages without forcing
all users into advisor-only assumptions.

Reason:
Forge already spans Candidate, Precontract, Advisor, Manager and Partner
contexts.

Risk:
Scope may become too broad without phased ownership.

LOCK CANDIDATE 15

The real competitor is fragmented career management: Excel, CRM, manual
processes, human memory and decision uncertainty.

Reason:
This correctly frames the product problem as fragmentation, not only CRM
replacement.

Risk:
Competitive framing should remain architectural, not marketing.

RECOMMENDED LOCK PRIORITY

Priority 1:
Career stage is a first-class architectural context.

Priority 2:
Manager Intelligence exists to develop people, not surveil them.

Priority 3:
Purpose is human context, not a commercial pressure mechanism.

Priority 4:
Forge converts career context into decisions and action paths.

Priority 5:
Forge is a Career Operating System.

WHY THIS ORDER

The strongest constitutional lock is not the label. It is the architecture under
the label.

Locking career stage, manager-development boundary, purpose protection and
evidence-based action first makes "Career Operating System" defensible.

FINAL RECOMMENDATION

Verdict:
CANDIDATE FOR LOCK.

Do not ratify yet.

Recommended next PAQ:
Career Lifecycle Model and Career Stage Ownership Discovery.
