FORGE CAREER STAGE LOCK CANDIDATES

Status:
CONSTITUTIONAL LOCK CANDIDATES / NOT APPROVED

Purpose:
List candidate constitutional statements for Career Lifecycle and Career Stage.
These are not ratified.

LOCK CANDIDATE 1

Career Stage is a first-class architectural primitive.

Reason:
Career Stage changes how Forge interprets behavior, forecast, compensation,
planning, onboarding, partner context and manager development.

Risk:
Requires canonical ownership and evidence contract.

LOCK CANDIDATE 2

Career Stage is not title, compensation, production or org chart.

Reason:
Any one of those signals alone can misclassify a person.

Risk:
Local companies may expect their hierarchy to be treated as truth.

LOCK CANDIDATE 3

Forge separates Career Stage, Operating State and Leadership Stage.

Reason:
This prevents inactive advisors, managers without teams and partners without
leadership roles from being mis-modeled.

Risk:
The product may become more complex unless surfaced progressively.

LOCK CANDIDATE 4

The canonical Career Stage model is:
Candidate, Precontract, Advisor, Professional Advisor, Partner, Senior Partner,
Managing Partner.

Reason:
This captures entry, readiness, early operation, maturity, leverage and senior
career contribution.

Risk:
Partner/Senior Partner/Managing Partner require evidence definitions before
implementation.

LOCK CANDIDATE 5

Leadership Stage is a separate dimension:
Individual Contributor, Manager, Subdirector, Director.

Reason:
Leadership responsibility can be gained, lost or paused independently from
career maturity.

Risk:
Local organizational labels must be mapped carefully.

LOCK CANDIDATE 6

Stage transitions require evidence, confidence and effective period.

Reason:
Stage changes affect sensitive guidance and manager interpretation.

Risk:
Without review workflow, ambiguous transitions may become brittle.

LOCK CANDIDATE 7

Regression is not a single decline.

Reason:
Forge must distinguish temporary risk, dormancy, reactivation and true stage
regression.

Risk:
Managers may want aggressive demotion logic for performance management.

LOCK CANDIDATE 8

Candidate identity survives applications, precontract cycles and reentry.

Reason:
RecruitIdentity is durable; attempts are events, not the person.

Risk:
Requires careful lifecycle history to avoid overwrites.

LOCK CANDIDATE 9

Success criteria are stage-specific.

Reason:
Candidate success is fit evidence, precontract success is readiness, advisor
success is consistency, partner success is leverage, manager success is people
development.

Risk:
Product teams may default to production-only because it is easier to measure.

LOCK CANDIDATE 10

Manager Intelligence consumes Career Stage and Leadership Stage to develop
people, not surveil them.

Reason:
Career OS increases manager context and therefore needs a strong constitutional
boundary.

Risk:
Dashboard culture can turn development signals into pressure.

LOCK CANDIDATE 11

Local organization hierarchy belongs in Organization Profiles or Rule Packs, not
Forge Core.

Reason:
Forge Core must remain universal.

Risk:
If this is not enforced, Forge becomes company-specific.

LOCK CANDIDATE 12

Career Stage must never be inferred from Purpose Snapshot alone.

Reason:
Purpose is human context, not stage evidence or motivation score.

Risk:
Purpose data can be misused if treated as performance psychology.

RECOMMENDED LOCK ORDER

Priority 1:
Forge separates Career Stage, Operating State and Leadership Stage.

Priority 2:
Stage transitions require evidence, confidence and effective period.

Priority 3:
Career Stage is not title, compensation, production or org chart.

Priority 4:
Success criteria are stage-specific.

Priority 5:
Career Stage is a first-class architectural primitive.

Priority 6:
The canonical Career Stage model is Candidate, Precontract, Advisor,
Professional Advisor, Partner, Senior Partner, Managing Partner.

WHY THIS ORDER

Locking separation and evidence first is safer than locking stage names first.

The names matter, but the constitutional protection is the architecture:
separate dimensions, evidence, confidence and stage-specific success.

FINAL VERDICT

CANDIDATE FOR LOCK.

Reason:
The lifecycle model is now coherent enough to support Career OS as a serious
constitutional candidate, but final lock should wait for a formal Career Stage
Evidence Contract and ownership decision.
