## LAW CANDIDATE 1

Name:

Need Recognition Before Recommendation

Description:

A recommendation should not lead the conversation until the prospect has
recognized a relevant need, risk or desired outcome.

Evidence:

- Pedro discusses risk categories and risk transfer before product mapping.
- SMNYL uses ANF to identify the most important need.
- Kinder makes helping the prospect recognize need part of the second sale.

Sources:

Pedro Camarena Intelligence; Corporate Methodology; Kinder Methodology.

Confidence Level:

HIGH

## LAW CANDIDATE 4

Name:

Qualification Is More Than Contactability

Description:

A qualified prospect requires more than name and phone number. Qualification
requires relevant need, access, economic capacity, decision authority or
influence, and willingness to engage.

Evidence:

- SMNYL uses NAST: need, insurability / assurance, solvency and decision access.
- Kinder distinguishes contact / referred qualified from prospect qualified.
- Pedro requires an administrable risk, not merely a reachable person.

Sources:

Pedro Camarena Intelligence; Corporate Methodology; Kinder Methodology.

Confidence Level:

HIGH
